MEMORA

Smart Reminder & Task Manager for ADHD and Dementia

About Memora

Memora is designed to help individuals with ADHD and early-stage dementia stay independent and organized. It offers clear, calm reminders for important daily tasks such as medication, meals, and appointments.

Key Features

  • Smart reminders with adaptive timing
  • Voice and speech-to-text support
  • Caregiver alerts and shared monitoring
  • Gentle, sensory-friendly design
  • AI-assisted task suggestions
